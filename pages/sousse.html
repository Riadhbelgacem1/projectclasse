Sousse

Charming Coastal City

Overview

Sousse, located on the Gulf of Hammamet, is a vibrant coastal city with a rich history and a mix of modern and traditional elements.

Medina and Old Town

Explore the narrow alleys of Sousse's medina, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Admire the white-washed buildings, bustling souks, and historic landmarks.

Beaches and Promenade

Sousse's sandy beaches are perfect for sunbathing and swimming. Take a leisurely stroll along the palm-lined promenade and enjoy the sea views.
